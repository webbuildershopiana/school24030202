Our Curriculum

With over 18 years of expertise in crafting age-appropriate, activity-based curriculum, our meticulously researched and scientifically designed proprietary program caters to children aged 15 months to 6 years. In collaboration with leading corporations such as Genpact, IBM, Google, and others, our curriculum has been successfully implemented in metropolitan areas like Hyderabad and Secunderabad.

At Building brains, we believe in nurturing the whole child by providing a dynamic and experiential curriculum that celebrates the uniqueness of each learner. Our curriculum is designed with a focus on multiple intelligences, promoting holistic development and fostering a love for learning from an early age.

Aligned with the principles of the National Education Policy 2020, our curriculum emphasizes the development of life skills and emotional intelligence, preparing children for success in school and beyond. Through hands-on experiences, scientific experiments, exposure to nature, and sensory-stimulated activities, we create a rich learning environment that ignites curiosity and sparks creativity.

At Building Brains we are committed to providing a nurturing and enriching learning experience that empowers children to reach their full potential. Our curriculum is continuously evolving to meet the needs of our students and reflect the latest research in early childhood education. We invite you to explore our curriculum further and discover the endless possibilities for your child's growth and development.
