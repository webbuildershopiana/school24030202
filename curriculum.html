Curriculum

Fostering Language Development through Scientific Approaches

At Building Brains Preschool, we believe that language development lays the foundation for a child's future academic success. Through our scientifically-backed methodologies, we nurture young minds to become confident communicators, equipped with a rich vocabulary and a deep understanding of linguistic concepts.
In our playgroup program, children are immersed in a stimulating environment where they learn through the touch, feel, and say method. Children are exposed to over 500 words of vocabulary. From identifying objects to expressing their thoughts, every interaction becomes an opportunity to expand their linguistic horizons.
In nursery, we embark on the journey of literacy by focusing on fundamental skills such as phonics and blending. Our students start blending basic sounds and are introduced to reading three-letter words.
As our students progress to Senior KG, they delve deeper into the nuances of language. They not only grasp concepts such as nouns and pronouns but also develop a deeper understanding of language structures and conventions. Our curriculum is designed to cultivate critical thinking and analytical skills, empowering children to express themselves with clarity and precision.

Nurturing Mathematical Minds through Hands-On Learning

At Building Brains Preschool, we believe that mathematics is not just about numbers and equations; it's about developing a deep understanding of concepts and building problem-solving skills from an early age. Our innovative approach to mathematics education focuses on cognition and pre-math concepts, empowering children to explore, discover, and learn through meaningful activities and hands-on experiences.
Through a variety of sensory-rich activities, children develop a strong conceptual understanding of numbers, shapes, patterns, and spatial relationships. From counting and sorting to measuring and problem-solving, every activity is an opportunity for children to engage with mathematical concepts in a hands-on way.
Our approach to mathematics education goes beyond rote memorization and drills. We focus on developing students' reasoning skills by encouraging them to think critically, make connections, and solve problems creatively. By fostering a growth mindset and instilling confidence in their mathematical abilities, we empower children to tackle challenges with ease and enthusiasm.

Cultivating Life Skills & Social Skills

We recognize that education extends far beyond academics. That's why we are dedicated to nurturing not only the minds but also the hearts and hands of our students. Through a comprehensive life skills program, we empower children to navigate the world with confidence, independence, and resilience.
Hands-On Life Skills: From Brushing to Buttoning
From the moment children enter our doors, they embark on a journey of practical learning and self-discovery. Our hands-on approach to life skills education encompasses a wide range of activities, including brushing, combing, stacking, cleaning, zipping, buttoning, and more. Through daily routines and structured tasks, children develop essential motor skills and self-care habits that lay the foundation for a lifetime of independence.
Social Play and Conversation
In addition to practical life skills, we prioritize the development of social skills that are vital for success in both personal and professional spheres. Through pretend play, role play, conversation, and storytelling, children learn the art of communication, cooperation, empathy, and problem-solving. Whether they're playing house in our dramatic play area or engaging in group discussions during circle time, every interaction is an opportunity to practice social skills in a supportive and nurturing environment.

Exploring Nature and Beyond

we foster curiosity and exploration through a rich curriculum designed to connect children with the world around them. Our approach goes beyond traditional learning, encompassing natural concepts like plants, animals, the human body, transportation, and much more. Through hands-on activities like planting seeds, observing plant growth, and exploring different types of flora, through interactive lessons, engaging stories, and visits from animal friends we believe that every child is a natural-born explorer, eager to learn and discover. By integrating natural concepts into our curriculum, we inspire a lifelong love of learning and nurture the next generation of curious minds.

Physical Development in Kindergarten

We prioritize the holistic development of every child, including their physical well-being. Our curriculum is designed to promote both gross and fine motor skills through a variety of engaging activities and experiences.
Through active play, kids develop balance, coordination, and strength while enjoying the fresh air and sunshine.
We set up obstacle courses indoors and outdoors to challenge children's gross motor skills. Crawling under tunnels, balancing on beams, and navigating through obstacles help them improve spatial awareness and motor planning.
Our art corner is filled with materials that allow children to explore their creativity while developing fine motor skills. From cutting and pasting to drawing and painting, kids strengthen their hand-eye coordination and dexterity.
We provide a variety of toys and materials that promote fine motor development, such as building blocks, puzzles, and playdough. These hands-on activities help children refine their grasp, finger strength, and hand control.
Kindergarteners engage in activities that prepare them for writing, such as tracing lines, forming letters, and cutting with scissors. These exercises build the hand muscles and coordination needed for writing and drawing.
