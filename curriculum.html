Our Curriculum

With over 18 years of expertise in crafting age-appropriate, activity-based curriculum, our meticulously researched and scientifically designed proprietary program caters to children aged 15 months to 6 years. In collaboration with leading corporations such as Genpact, IBM, Google, and others, our curriculum has been successfully implemented in metropolitan areas like Hyderabad and Secunderabad.

At Building brains, we believe in nurturing the whole child by providing a dynamic and experiential curriculum that celebrates the uniqueness of each learner. Our curriculum is designed with a focus on multiple intelligences, promoting holistic development and fostering a love for learning from an early age.

Aligned with the principles of the National Education Policy 2020, our curriculum emphasizes the development of life skills and emotional intelligence, preparing children for success in school and beyond. Through hands-on experiences, scientific experiments, exposure to nature, and sensory-stimulated activities, we create a rich learning environment that ignites curiosity and sparks creativity.

Social & Emotional

Nurturing both hearts and minds for holistic growth.

At our school, we prioritize social and emotional development, fostering a supportive environment where both hearts and minds are nurtured. Through holistic approaches, we aim to cultivate well-rounded individuals who thrive academically and emotionally, preparing them for success in all aspects of life.

Fine & Gross Motor

Developing fine and gross motor skills for physical confidence and coordination.

Empowering students through comprehensive fine and gross motor skill development, paving the path for enhanced physical confidence, coordination and overall well-being. By integrating fun and engaging activities, we foster a supportive environment where students can thrive physically, emotionally, and academically.

Skills

Embracing skill development as a cornerstone, our school cultivates students' abilities to navigate the complexities of today's dynamic world. Our commitment to fostering a culture of continuous growth ensures that each student emerges prepared for success in a rapidly evolving society.

Embarking on journeys through the realms of social dynamics and natural wonders, we ignite curiosity and critical thought in our students. Through immersive learning, we cultivate profound insights into our interconnected world, empowering them as catalysts for positive change.

Cognitive/ Pre-math

Building cognitive abilities and introducing foundational math concepts.

Explore the wonders of cognitive growth and early math discovery with us, as young minds flourish and numbers come alive. Join our journey of exploration and watch as curiosity leads to new insights and mathematical mastery blossoms.
