At Building Brains we are committed to providing a nurturing and enriching learning experience that empowers children to reach their full potential. Our curriculum is continuously evolving to meet the needs of our students and reflect the latest research in early childhood education. We invite you to explore our curriculum further and discover the endless possibilities for your child's growth and development.

Multiple Intelligences Approach

Recognizing that every child learns differently, our curriculum caters to diverse learning styles by incorporating activities that engage various intelligences, including linguistic, logical-mathematical, spatial, bodily-kinesthetic, musical, interpersonal, intrapersonal, and naturalistic intelligences.

Experiential Learning

We believe that children learn best through active exploration and discovery. Our curriculum is experiential in nature, providing opportunities for hands-on learning, experimentation, and problem-solving that encourage critical thinking and creativity.

Nature-Based Learning

Our curriculum includes regular outdoor experiences and nature-based activities that foster a deep connection with the natural world. Through outdoor exploration, gardening, and nature walks, children develop an appreciation for the environment and gain valuable sensory experiences.

Promotion of Life Skills and Emotional Intelligence

In addition to academic skills, we place a strong emphasis on the development of essential life skills such as communication, collaboration, resilience, and empathy. Our curriculum includes activities and lessons that promote emotional intelligence, self-awareness, and social-emotional competence.

Reading Readiness

Our curriculum has yielded excellent results in developing early literacy skills, with a focus on phonics, vocabulary development, comprehension, and a love for reading. Through storytelling, dramatic play, and interactive literacy activities, we lay a strong foundation for lifelong reading success.
