About us

Learn More About Our Work And Our Cultural Activities

Welcome to our school, where our mission extends far beyond traditional education. Alongside our rigorous academic curriculum, we prioritize the cultivation of a vibrant cultural community. Through a diverse range of initiatives, including art exhibitions, performances, workshops and more, we foster an environment where creativity thrives and students can explore their passions beyond the classroom.

Our commitment to cultural enrichment is integral to our ethos of holistic education. By engaging with various forms of expression, students not only broaden their horizons but also develop empathy, critical thinking skills and a deeper understanding of the world around them. We invite you to delve into the heart of our school community, where learning transcends boundaries and where every student has the opportunity to flourish academically, artistically and socially.

Our Board of Directors

Sonika Maharwal

Trained in Neuro level programming and Early Childhood, alumni of IMT Ghaziabad Ms. Sonika has nurtured over 10,000 students in the past 12 years of being an educationist and early childhood specialist. She has been Awarded as the educationist of the year 2023, She has conducted various National and International workshops for educators and parents over these years.

Ms. Anita Rao

is the Founding Director and the Managing Director of Educational Catalysts India Pvt. Ltd. She has done her Management studies from Indian Institute of Management, Kolkatta. She is trained in “Total Quality Control in School Management” by the Confederation of Indian Industries. She is also a member of NAEYC (National Association for the Education of Young Children) USA

Rashi Gupta

With over 35 years of experience in the field of education, Mrs. Gupta has a deep understanding of curriculum and methodologies. Being a certified ISO auditor, she specialises in quality training of teachers and culturally specific curriculum. She believes in stress free academics through innovative teaching techniques.
